Influence maximization based on history

Once we discover the user history on the social network, we calculate an impact coefficient.
This impact coefficient is the probability that a given user can influence another one.
The more active you were on Yelp.. the more probability you have to influence other people!

A community based approach

We use popular community detection algorithms to discover communities among the social network itself.
We study the communities itself and found the most influential user of a given community
Biggest communities are our main focus and concern! The bigger the community, the more I want to target it for analysis
